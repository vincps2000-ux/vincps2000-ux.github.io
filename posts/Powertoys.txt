Had an Idea for a COPY&Paste Gadget based on OCR on Screenshots.
After realising Windows Powertoys already has this, I looked at the automations more. 


Text Extraxtor: Copy based on OCR in a selected Area. Works on any dispalyed Text.
Expanded Paste: Allows Pasting in as specific Format. The Default-Text is goated for pasting from places with heavy Styling.
New+: Allows creating Schemas that are available under New. 
Registry-Editor: Allows editing Editing of Registry data.
ZoomIt: Activation of Zoom function
Enviromentvariables: Editor for Enviromentvariables. A bit Strange since the normal Editor is very much functional, just in a weirtd place.
