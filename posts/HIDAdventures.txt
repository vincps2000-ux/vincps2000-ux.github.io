Built small hid (human interface device) Inspector, with the goal to see mouse and keyboard input.
Used for this Project was pywinusb(https://github.com/rene-aguirre/pywinusb/tree/master).
Windows only makes mouse and keyboard availble through event System, raw hid is not visible.

Only Inputs looking like a alive bit(because of regular cadence and unchanging Payload) from an unknown hid were seen.(VID: 0x046D PID: 0xC52B)
(https://nodeloop.org/projects/usb-identifier/) allowed Identification, though not supported on every browser (webusb is needed).
It was my Logitech USB Receiver.
