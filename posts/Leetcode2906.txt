1. Approach: get Product of whole matrix, divide by current Cell.
Does not work, because: Negative Numbers, max int. 

2. Approach: 
a forward pass over the matrix, calculating every product "before" the item.
a backward pass over the matrix, calculating every product "after" the item.
then: forward pass value * backward pass value modulo 12345.

Interesting: The int overflows on some testcases. 
Soloution: modulo can be applied multiple times, and order doesnt matter with multiplication operations:
(a⋅b)modm=((amodm)⋅(bmodm))modm
und 
(amodm)modm=amodm
So applying modulo after every operation reduces overflow risk 
from the product of every matrix item to the product of the highest item * 12345.
