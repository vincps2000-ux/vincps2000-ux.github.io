Investigating Plant Uml, as a potential documenting Tool.

Using Webserver:
https://www.plantuml.com/
Extreme Basic Turtorial:
https://www.augmentedmind.de/2021/01/03/plantuml-tutorial-diagrams-as-code/
By far the most useful resource, as a wiki:
https://pdf.plantuml.net/1.2020.22/PlantUML_Language_Reference_Guide_en.pdf

usecases are defined like:
(class e.g. actor) (Caption) as (Name)
default is actor

relations are defined like:
(element1) (connector) (element2): (line description)

groups defined like:
group (name) 
[content]
end


Hierachy is from left to right, with - being equal, -- being down one, --- being down two, etc.

Result: Interesting, but needs to be used in real project to decide.
Prediction: high initial cost, medium payoff, good for people fast on keyboard, and working on unclear architecture
