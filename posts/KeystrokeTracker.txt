Project Goal is to collect Statistics about my Activity during Projects.
Interesting Discovery: 
When interacting with Bitwarden, Keystrokes are counted.

Key Code that allows reacting keyboard: 
SetWindowsHookEx(WH_KEYBOARD_LL, _proc,GetModuleHandle(mod.ModuleName), 0);
Further Info: 
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/api/winuser/nf-winuser-setwindowshookexa
Hook ID(WH_KEYBOARD_LL) is 13:
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/winmsg/lowlevelkeyboardproc
