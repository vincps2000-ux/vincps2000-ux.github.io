Looked at Latex in VSC.

Distribution used: (https://miktex.org/)

Minus: No easy single click Version for VSC use(However, VSC is not meant as a document editor).
Plus: .tex format focuses more on Information contained in Document, instead of Presentation.

Latex is more like a html Resource than a fully defined Document like word. 

Some Examples:

\title \date \author serves as so called preamble data.

\begin{document}
\end{document}
    used as bounding marker for whole document.

\pagenumbering{arabic|roman|gobble(no numbers)}
    sets the way pagenumbers are displayed
